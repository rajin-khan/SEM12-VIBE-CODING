# NSU Grading Policy

## Grade Scale

| Grade | Points | Grade | Points |
|-------|--------|-------|--------|
| A     | 4.0    | C     | 2.0    |
| A-    | 3.7    | C-    | 1.7    |
| B+    | 3.3    | D+    | 1.3    |
| B     | 3.0    | D     | 1.0    |
| B-    | 2.7    | F     | 0.0    |
| C+    | 2.3    |       |        |

## Special Grades

- **W** (Withdrawn): Excluded from CGPA and credits attempted
- **I** (Incomplete): Treated as F (0.0) until resolved
- **T** (Transfer/Waived): Credit earned, excluded from CGPA
- **P** (Pass): For 0-credit courses; no GPA impact

## Retake Policy

- Students may retake courses with grade B or lower
- Only the best grade is used for CGPA calculation
- F grade remains in CGPA until retaken with a better grade

## Probation Policy

- P1: First semester with cumulative CGPA below 2.0
- P2: Second consecutive semester below 2.0
- Dismissal: Third consecutive semester below 2.0
